Our Goals

To ensure efficient poultry management, from feeds distribution to egg sales monitoring.

PoultryMetrix helps us modernize how Bocago Poultry Farm runs its daily operations—reducing errors, saving time, and boosting productivity.

We aim to provide transparency across sales, stock, and payroll, ensuring smooth management for both farm owners and staff.

Our Work

From managing layer feeds to tracking chick growth, we combine traditional poultry practices with modern monitoring tools.

PoultryMetrix automates critical workflows: inventory, sales, and payroll—all under one system.

Our work is not just about farming chickens; it’s about building a sustainable poultry business powered by reliable data.

Our Passion

We are passionate about poultry farming and the technology that makes it smarter and more efficient.

PoultryMetrix was created to empower farms like Bocago Poultry with real-time insights.

Healthy flocks, happy customers, and a sustainable future—that’s what drives our passion every day.
